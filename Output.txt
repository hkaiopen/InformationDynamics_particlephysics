All extractions give p_ID ≈ 1 (information superconductor limit).
The reactor neutrino measurement is used as the p_ID = 1 calibration anchor.
Hyperon decay and muon g-2 results are consistent with unity within errors.

collective excitations occur in a nearly perfect information superconductor.
